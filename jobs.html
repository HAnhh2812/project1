Network Administrator (Ref: NA101)

Reports to: IT Infrastructure Manager

Salary Range: $80,000 - $95,000 per annum

The Network Administrator is responsible for maintaining, securing, and optimizing the company's internal network infrastructure. This role ensures consistent connectivity, fast performance, and data protection across all departments. The successful candidate will also manage network troubleshooting, monitor traffic, and assist in upgrading systems to meet the company's operational goals.

Key Responsibilities

  • Monitor and maintain LAN, WAN, and wireless network systems.
  • Configure and manage routers, switches, and firewalls.
  • Perform routine network backups and disaster recovery testing.
  • Troubleshoot network connectivity issues and outages.
  • Collaborate with the IT Security team to enforce access control policies.

Essential Skills and Qualifications

  1. Bachelor's degree in Information Technology, Computer Science, or a related discipline.
  2. Minimum 2 years of hands-on experience in network administration.
  3. Strong knowledge of TCP/IP, DNS, DHCP, and VPN technologies.
  4. Ability to configure Cisco or similar enterprise-grade network devices.
  5. Excellent analytical, documentation, and communication skills.

Preferable Attributes

  • Professional certifications such as CCNA, CCNP, or CompTIA Network+.
  • Experience with cloud-based networking (AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud).
  • Familiarity with network automation tools and scripting (e.g., Python, Ansible).

Back-End Developer (Ref: BE204)

Reports to: Software Development Manager

Salary Range:$85,000 - $100,000 per annum

The Back-End Developer is responsible for building and maintaining the server-side logic, databases, and APIs that power the company's applications. The role focuses on ensuring reliability, security, and scalability of all backend systems while supporting the integration between front-end interfaces and data services. The successful candidate will collaborate closely with front-end developers, DevOps engineers, and system architects to deliver robust and efficient software solutions.

Key Responsibilities

  • Develop, test, and deploy server-side applications using modern frameworks.
  • Design and optimize relational and NoSQL databases.
  • Build and maintain secure APIs and web services.
  • Collaborate with front-end developers to ensure seamless data flow.
  • Troubleshoot and debug server-side performance or security issues.

Essential Skills and Qualifications

  1. Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or related discipline.
  2. Minimum 2 years of experience in back-end web development.
  3. Proficiency in one or more programming languages such as Node.js, Python, or Java.
  4. Solid understanding of RESTful APIs and database management systems (MySQL, MongoDB, or PostgreSQL).
  5. Experience with version control systems such as Git and collaborative development tools.

Preferable Attributes

  • Experience with containerization technologies such as Docker or Kubernetes.
  • Familiarity with cloud deployment (AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud).
  • Knowledge of security best practices and authentication frameworks (OAuth, JWT).

UI/UX Designer (Ref: UX307)

Reports to: Creative Director / Product Design Lead

Salary Range:$75,000 - $90,000 per annum

The UI/UX Designer will be responsible for creating intuitive, visually appealing, and user-centered digital interfaces. The role involves understanding user needs, developing wireframes and prototypes, and collaborating with developers to ensure consistent design execution. The successful candidate will transform complex ideas into seamless user experiences that align with the company's brand identity and accessibility standards.

Key Responsibilities

  • Conduct user research, interviews, and usability testing.
  • Create wireframes, mockups, and interactive prototypes.
  • Develop and maintain consistent design systems and style guides.
  • Collaborate with developers to implement responsive and accessible designs.
  • Continuously evaluate and improve user experience based on feedback and analytics.

Essential Skills and Qualifications

  1. Bachelor's degree in Graphic Design, Interaction Design, or a related field.
  2. At least 2 years of experience in UI/UX design or product design.
  3. Proficiency in design tools such as Figma, Adobe XD, or Sketch.
  4. Strong understanding of user-centered design principles and accessibility guidelines (WCAG).
  5. Excellent visual communication and problem-solving skills.

Preferable Attributes

  • Experience in front-end development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript).
  • Knowledge of motion design or micro-interactions.
  • Familiarity with design thinking and agile workflow.

Machine Learning Engineer (Ref: ML451)

Reports to: Head of Artificial Intelligence Department

Salary Range:$95,000 - $120,000 per annum

The Machine Learning Engineer will design, develop, and deploy intelligent systems that enable data-driven decision-making across the organization. The role involves building predictive models, optimizing algorithms, and collaborating with data scientists to transform research into scalable production solutions. The ideal candidate will possess strong coding skills, deep understanding of statistical learning, and a passion for innovation in artificial intelligence.

Key Responsibilities

  • Develop and maintain machine learning models for various business applications.
  • Work with large datasets to extract insights and improve model performance.
  • Implement, test, and deploy ML pipelines in production environments.
  • Collaborate with software engineers and data scientists on scalable solutions.
  • Continuously research and evaluate emerging ML frameworks and technologies.

Essential Skills and Qualifications

  1. Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Data Science, or a related field.
  2. At least 3 years of experience in machine learning model development.
  3. Proficiency in Python and ML libraries such as TensorFlow, PyTorch, or Scikit-learn.
  4. Strong grasp of statistics, data preprocessing, and feature engineering.
  5. Experience with cloud-based ML tools (AWS Sagemaker, Google Vertex AI, or Azure ML).

Preferable Attributes

  • Experience with deep learning architectures (CNN, RNN, Transformers).
  • Knowledge of MLOps and CI/CD deployment pipelines.
  • Publications or contributions to open-source AI projects.
